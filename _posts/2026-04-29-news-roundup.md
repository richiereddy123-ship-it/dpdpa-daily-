## India: DPDPA Takes Effect

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its accompanying 2025 rules, has officially come into force, marking a significant milestone for the country's data privacy landscape. This full operationalization initiates a new era of compliance obligations for data fiduciaries and processors, with various publications offering guidance on its practical implementation and principles. Legal experts and industry bodies are now focused on understanding how the rules will be put into practice, comparing India's "middle path" approach to global privacy paradigms like the GDPR.
