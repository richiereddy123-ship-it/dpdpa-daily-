## India: DPDPA Fully Activated

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) has officially come into full effect, with the government notifying the comprehensive DPDP Rules 2025. This landmark development establishes the foundational legal framework for personal data protection across the nation, setting new standards for data fiduciaries and empowering data principals. The activation marks a new era for data privacy enforcement in India, necessitating immediate attention from all entities handling personal data.
