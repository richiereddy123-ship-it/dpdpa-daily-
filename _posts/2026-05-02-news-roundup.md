## India: DPDPA Takes Effect & Legal Challenges

India's landmark Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its associated 2025 rules, has officially come into effect. This marks a significant shift in the country's data privacy landscape, ushering in new compliance requirements for data fiduciaries and processors.
