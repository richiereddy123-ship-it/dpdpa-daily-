## India: Regulatory & Legal Developments

*   **Supreme Court Issues Notice on DPDPA Challenge:** The Supreme Court of India has issued notice on a constitutional challenge to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and the recently finalized Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025. This development signals potential judicial scrutiny of the new privacy framework, adding a layer of complexity to its implementation.
