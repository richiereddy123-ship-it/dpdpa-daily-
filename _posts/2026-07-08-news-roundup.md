## India: Regulatory & Board Updates

MeitY has officially initiated the process to staff the crucial Data Protection Board of India (DPBI), inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This marks a significant step towards operationalizing the DPDPA's enforcement mechanism, essential for adjudicating compliance and imposing penalties under the Act.
