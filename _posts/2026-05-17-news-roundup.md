## India: DPDPA Legal Challenges
The Supreme Court has issued notices on several petitions challenging the constitutionality of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025. These challenges, brought forth by groups including the Internet Freedom Foundation and Reporters Collective, raise concerns about the Act's implications for fundamental rights and transparency, particularly its amendments affecting the Right to Information Act.
