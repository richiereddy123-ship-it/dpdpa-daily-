## India: DPDPA Takes Effect & Board Formation

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023, has officially come into force following the finalization and notification of the DPDP Rules, 2025. This marks a pivotal moment for data privacy in the country, establishing a comprehensive legal framework for the processing of digital personal data and signaling an urgent need for businesses to accelerate their compliance efforts.
