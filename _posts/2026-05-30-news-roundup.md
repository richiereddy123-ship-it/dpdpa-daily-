## India: DPDPA Implementation & Readiness

The urgency for Indian enterprises to secure data at its source and prepare for DPDPA compliance is being widely emphasized as the implementation clock ticks. Companies like Scalefusion are highlighting the importance of endpoint-centric security strategies to meet these new regulatory demands. Additionally, new solutions are emerging, with GoTrust launching an automated consent management platform specifically designed to aid DPDPA compliance for businesses. However, concerns remain about the readiness of startups to shoulder the significant compliance burden introduced by the DPDPA.
