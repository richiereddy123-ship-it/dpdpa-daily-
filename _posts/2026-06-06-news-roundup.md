## India: Regulatory Updates and Compliance Focus

The UT Chandigarh administration recently organized a workshop to enhance understanding and compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023, among government and private sector stakeholders. This initiative reflects a broader push for DPDPA awareness and readiness across various regions. Simultaneously, Indian enterprises are being strongly advised to prioritize securing personal data at the source as the DPDPA implementation deadline draws nearer, emphasizing proactive data security measures. To assist businesses, SaralPrivacy has launched a free readiness assessment tool, helping companies identify and address privacy compliance gaps.
