## India: Regulatory Updates

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has initiated the process of establishing the Data Protection Board of India by inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This critical step moves the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) closer to full operationalization, with the Board poised to become the primary enforcement and adjudication body. MeitY is also actively seeking industry feedback on proposals to potentially shorten the compliance timelines for the DPDPA, indicating a flexible approach to implementation based on business practicalities.
