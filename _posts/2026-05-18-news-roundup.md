## India: DPDPA Takes Force Amidst Challenges

*   **Digital Personal Data Protection Act and Rules Now in Effect:** India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules 2025, has officially entered into force. This landmark legislation introduces India's first comprehensive data protection framework, establishing new standards for data fiduciaries regarding personal data processing, consent, and accountability.
