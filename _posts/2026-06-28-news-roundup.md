## India: DPDPA Fully Operational
India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 has officially come into full effect following the finalization and notification of its accompanying Rules in 2025. This landmark legislation, along with its detailed regulations, now governs personal data processing across the country, marking a significant shift in India's privacy landscape. Businesses are urged to prioritize data security at the source to ensure compliance.
