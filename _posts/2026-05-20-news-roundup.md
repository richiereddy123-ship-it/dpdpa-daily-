## India: DPDPA Litigation & Constitutional Challenges
The Supreme Court is preparing to hear multiple petitions challenging the constitutionality of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 and its associated rules, 2025. These challenges, including those filed by the Internet Freedom Foundation and the Reporters Collective, question various aspects of the new data protection framework, including its impact on the Right to Information Act. The court has issued notices, indicating a significant legal review of the Act's foundational principles.
