## India: Regulatory Updates and Compliance Focus

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has officially invited applications for the chairperson and members of the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI). This significant step signals the government's intent to operationalize the enforcement mechanism for the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023. Concurrently, IDfy has won the MeitY–NeGD DPDP Innovation Challenge, highlighting government efforts to foster technological solutions for privacy enforcement.
