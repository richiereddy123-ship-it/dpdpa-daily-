## India: DPDPA Implementation and Compliance

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) and its accompanying Rules, now fully effective, are compelling significant operational overhauls for businesses across various sectors. Experts emphasize the critical need for robust compliance frameworks, particularly for startups, adtech platforms, and the banking sector, to navigate new consent management, data fiduciary responsibilities, and updated cross-border data transfer protocols. Companies are actively assessing the operational impacts and designing DPDPA-compliant interfaces, especially for e-commerce platforms, to meet the new legal standards.
