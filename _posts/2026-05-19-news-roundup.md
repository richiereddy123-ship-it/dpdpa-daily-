## India: DPDPA Legal Challenges
The Supreme Court of India is actively considering multiple petitions that challenge the constitutionality of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) and its recently notified rules. Various civil society groups and media organizations, including the Internet Freedom Foundation and the Reporters Collective, have expressed concerns regarding the Act's potential impact on fundamental rights and its interface with existing transparency laws. This judicial review is a pivotal moment for India's new data protection regime as its legal foundations are scrutinized.
