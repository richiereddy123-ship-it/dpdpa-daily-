## India: DPDPA Implementation and Compliance

Indian enterprises are urged to prioritize data security at the source as the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) implementation timeline progresses. The Act establishes India's first comprehensive data protection framework, shifting how organizations collect and utilize personal data across various sectors, including digital education.
