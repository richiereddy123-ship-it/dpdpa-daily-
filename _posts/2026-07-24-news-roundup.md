## India: Regulatory Updates & Litigation
The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has initiated the process to staff the Data Protection Board of India, inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This is a crucial step towards making the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) fully operational and establishing the key enforcement body.
