## India: DPDPA Implementation and Challenges

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its accompanying 2025 Rules, has officially come into force, marking a significant milestone in the nation's data privacy landscape. This full implementation means that data fiduciaries must now navigate the new compliance requirements, which include updated definitions, lawful processing grounds, and operational impacts across various sectors like banking and adtech.
