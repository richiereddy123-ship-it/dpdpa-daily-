## India: DPDPA Takes Effect
India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 has officially come into force, following the finalization and notification of its accompanying rules in 2025. This marks a significant milestone in India's data protection landscape, establishing a legal framework for the processing of digital personal data. The Act's implementation introduces new obligations for data fiduciaries and outlines the powers of the upcoming Data Protection Board.
