## India: DPDPA Enters Force & Rules Finalized

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 is now fully operational, with the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules 2025 having been finalized and published. This marks a significant shift in India's data privacy landscape, providing the framework for enforcement and compliance across various sectors. The rules offer crucial guidance for organizations on implementing the Act's provisions.
