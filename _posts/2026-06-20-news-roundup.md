## India: Data Protection Board & Legal Challenges

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has initiated the process for establishing India's Data Protection Board, inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This move is a critical step towards operationalizing the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, setting the stage for enforcement of the new privacy regime.
