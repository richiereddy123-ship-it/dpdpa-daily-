## India: Regulatory & Enforcement Developments

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has officially opened applications for the Chairperson and members of the Data Protection Board of India, signaling a crucial step towards operationalizing the DPDPA's enforcement mechanism. This move comes as MeitY also seeks industry feedback on potentially shortening the compliance timelines for the Act, indicating a push for quicker adoption. Furthermore, MeitY's recent innovation challenge, won by IDfy for its privacy-enhancing solutions, underscores the government's focus on fostering technological readiness for privacy enforcement.

## India: DPDPA Implementation & Sectoral Impact

Indian enterprises are urged to prioritize securing data at its source and to understand the operational impacts of the DPDPA, as the compliance clock continues to tick. The Act introduces a unified data protection framework for the first time in India, necessitating fundamental changes in how data is collected and used. This includes navigating the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules 2025, which provide detailed guidelines.

The intersection of DPDPA with Artificial Intelligence (AI) also raises complex questions about data processing and accountability for emerging technologies.
