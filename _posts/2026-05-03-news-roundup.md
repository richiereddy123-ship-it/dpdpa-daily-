## India: DPDPA Takes Effect

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 has officially come into force, following the finalization and notification of its accompanying rules in 2025. This marks a significant milestone in India's data privacy landscape, establishing a comprehensive legal framework for personal data processing. The new rules provide granular details on how the Act's principles will be implemented, offering essential guidance for data fiduciaries and data principals.
