## India: Regulatory Updates & Compliance
The Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025, have been released, providing detailed operational guidelines for the DPDPA and offering a clearer roadmap for compliance. These rules outline specific requirements for data fiduciaries and processors, moving India closer to full privacy enforcement.
