## India: Regulatory Updates & Implementation

IT Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw has announced the finalization of the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025, with their official publication anticipated in the coming days. This development is a crucial step towards operationalizing the DPDPA, providing much-needed clarity on compliance requirements for data fiduciaries.
