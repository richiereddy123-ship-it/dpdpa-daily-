## India: DPDPA Enforcement & Litigation

MeitY has officially begun the process of constituting India's Data Protection Board, inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This crucial step signals the imminent operationalization of the DPDPA's enforcement mechanism, moving the country closer to a fully functional data privacy regime.
