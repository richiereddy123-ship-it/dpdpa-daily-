## India: DPDPA Implementation and Compliance

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) has officially come into force with the finalization of its rules, marking a significant shift for data fiduciaries across India. Businesses, particularly startups and those in the adtech sector, are now grappling with the substantial compliance burden, necessitating a move from data maximization to a trust-based approach. Technology providers like Netskope, iValue, and Varonis are responding by launching India-specific management planes and partnerships to support enterprises in achieving DPDPA compliance and data sovereignty.
