## India: Regulatory & Legal Updates

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has initiated the process of establishing the crucial Data Protection Board of India, inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This marks a significant step towards operationalizing the enforcement mechanism of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023.
