## India: DPDPA Takes Effect & Legal Challenges

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its accompanying Rules 2025, has officially come into force, marking a significant shift in the country's data privacy landscape. This full implementation follows the finalization of the rules, setting a new standard for data processing within India.
