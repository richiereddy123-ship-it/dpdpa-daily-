## India: Regulatory Milestones & Compliance Urgency

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has announced that the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Rules are now finalized and expected to be published shortly. This marks a critical step towards the full operationalization of India's landmark data protection law. Concurrently, MeitY has initiated the process to staff the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI), inviting applications for the chairperson and members, which is crucial for adjudicating DPDPA violations and enforcing compliance.
