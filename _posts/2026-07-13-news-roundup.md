## India: DPDPA Implementation & Readiness

**DPDPA Rules Finalized and Board Appointments Underway:** Union IT Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw announced that the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Rules 2025 have been finalized and are set for imminent publication, providing crucial clarity for compliance. Concurrently, MeitY has initiated the process of appointing the chairperson and members for the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI), a critical step towards establishing the Act's enforcement body.
