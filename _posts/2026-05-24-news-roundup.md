## India: DPDPA Implementation & Readiness

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) continues to be a focal point, with discussions emphasizing its imminent full implementation and the significant operational shifts it mandates for Indian enterprises. Analysts note that the DPDPA, along with the recently notified DPDPA Rules 2025, establishes India's first comprehensive data protection framework, fundamentally reshaping how organizations handle personal data. This new regime requires businesses to understand the practical application of the law, with comparisons being drawn to global privacy standards as India carves its own path.
