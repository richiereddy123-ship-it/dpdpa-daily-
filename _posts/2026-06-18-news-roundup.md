## India: Regulatory & Compliance Focus

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) continues to dominate discussions, with businesses actively seeking solutions and guidance for compliance. Netskope has launched its NewEdge Management Plane in India, aiming to assist enterprises in meeting DPDPA requirements and ensuring data sovereignty within the country. This comes as Oracle and other industry voices emphasize the urgent need for Indian businesses to secure data at the source to prepare for tightening enforcement.
