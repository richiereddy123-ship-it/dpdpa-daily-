## India: DPDPA Enactment and Compliance
The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its accompanying 2025 Rules, has officially come into force, establishing India's first comprehensive data protection framework. This marks a critical juncture for organizations, requiring immediate action to align data handling practices with the new legal requirements. Guidance from various firms emphasizes the operational impacts for businesses, including securing data at the source, managing cross-border data transfers, and establishing robust data breach response protocols, with the banking sector facing significant adjustments.
