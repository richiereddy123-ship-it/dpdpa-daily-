## India: DPDPA Takes Effect & Compliance Focus

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 has officially come into force following the finalization of its accompanying rules in 2025. This landmark legislation marks a significant shift in India's data privacy landscape, ushering in a new era of enforcement. Indian enterprises are urged to prioritize data security at the source, as the DPDPA's implementation clock is now actively ticking, requiring a fundamental understanding of the Act's provisions and the new 2025 rules for compliance.
