## India: DPDPA Enactment & Rules
The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its finalized 2025 rules, has officially come into force across India. This milestone establishes a comprehensive legal framework for personal data processing, outlining practical implementation guidelines for data fiduciaries and data principals, covering aspects from consent mechanisms to data breach notifications. The rules aim to balance individual privacy rights with the needs of the digital economy, guiding businesses on compliance.
