## India: Regulatory & Enforcement Milestones

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has initiated the process to establish India's crucial data protection authority, inviting applications for the chairperson and members of the Digital Personal Data Protection Board. This move signals a significant step towards operationalizing the enforcement mechanisms under the DPDPA. Separately, MeitY's NeGD DPDP Innovation Challenge concluded, with IDfy emerging as a winner, showcasing government efforts to foster technological solutions aligned with the upcoming privacy enforcement landscape.

## India: DPDPA Legal Challenges & Intersections

The Supreme Court of India has issued a notice regarding a constitutional challenge to both the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025. This significant development highlights ongoing legal scrutiny and potential future amendments to the framework. Additionally, the Reserve Bank of India's 2026 Model Risk Draft is being analyzed for its interplay with the DPDPA, underscoring the increasing convergence of data protection and financial sector regulations.

## India: DPDPA Compliance & Implementation Focus

Global Capability Centres (GDCs) operating in India face specific implications regarding the DPDPA's consent manager framework, requiring careful strategic planning. The Act's impact on the burgeoning med-tech sector is also a key discussion point, highlighting the need for tailored compliance strategies for sensitive health data.

## India: Critical Analysis of DPDPA

Experts continue to analyze the DPDPA, with discussions focusing on the interpretational uncertainty arising from the absence of a statutory definition for "harm." Concerns are also being raised about potential regulatory and structural blind spots within the current framework, suggesting areas that may require clarification or further development. Meanwhile, detailed recommendations are being offered for effective implementation, particularly for MSMEs, to ensure compliance is achievable across businesses of all sizes.
