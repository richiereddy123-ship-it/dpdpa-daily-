## India: DPDPA Rules Notified & Implementation Underway

The Indian government officially notified the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Rules, 2025, bringing the landmark Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) into full effect. These rules establish a comprehensive framework for data processing, empowering user privacy and setting new standards for data governance across the country. Implementation is slated to occur in phases over the next 12 to 18 months, allowing businesses time to adapt to the new legal landscape.
