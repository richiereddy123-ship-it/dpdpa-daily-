## India: Regulatory & Compliance Updates

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has officially opened applications for the chairperson and members of the Data Protection Board of India. This crucial development marks a significant step towards operationalizing the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) and establishing the regulatory body responsible for its enforcement.
