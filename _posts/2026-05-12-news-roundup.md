## India: DPDPA Legal Challenges

The Supreme Court is set to commence hearings on constitutional challenges against India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023, and its accompanying 2025 rules. Petitions filed by the Internet Freedom Foundation, Reporters Collective, and an RTI forum specifically question provisions perceived to dilute the Right to Information Act, raising concerns about transparency and digital rights. This marks a critical juncture for the new privacy framework, as its legality and scope will be scrutinized by the apex court.
