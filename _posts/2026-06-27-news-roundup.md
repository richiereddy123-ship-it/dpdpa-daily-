## India: DPDPA Takes Effect & Rules Finalized

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 has officially come into force, accompanied by the finalization and activation of the Digital Personal Data Protection Rules 2025. This marks a significant milestone, establishing a comprehensive legal framework for data privacy in the country and ushering in a new era of enforcement. Numerous analyses and guidance documents have emerged, dissecting the DPDP Rules 2025 and offering insights into their practical implementation and compliance requirements, highlighting India's "middle path" approach that balances global privacy paradigms with local considerations.
