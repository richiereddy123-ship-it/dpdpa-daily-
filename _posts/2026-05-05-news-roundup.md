## India: DPDPA Implementation & Compliance

With the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 and its accompanying Rules 2025 now officially in force, Indian businesses are actively navigating the new compliance landscape. Experts highlight significant operational impacts, particularly for startups and adtech platforms, urging them to prepare for increased compliance burdens related to data processing and consent management. Several technology firms are rolling out solutions, such as new management planes and partnerships, to assist enterprises in meeting DPDPA requirements. Discussions also continue on whether the DPDPA fosters a "data maximisation" regime and its implications for the future of enterprise AI in India.
