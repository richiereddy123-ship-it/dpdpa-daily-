## India: Regulatory & Compliance Updates

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has officially commenced the process of establishing the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI) by inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This crucial step marks significant progress towards fully operationalizing the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) and setting up its primary enforcement body.
