## India: DPDPA Enactment & Compliance

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its associated 2025 rules, has officially come into force, establishing India's first comprehensive data protection framework. This marks a significant shift for businesses, requiring immediate action to align data processing practices with the new regulations.
