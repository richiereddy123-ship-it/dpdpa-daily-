## India: DPDPA Implementation & Litigation

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023, along with its recently notified 2025 Rules, continues to dominate privacy discussions in India as the compliance deadline approaches. Businesses are feeling increased pressure to adapt, with new tools like SaralPrivacy's free readiness assessment and GoTrust's automated consent management platforms emerging to help enterprises identify and bridge privacy gaps. Experts emphasize the need for robust endpoint-centric security and securing data at the source to meet the Act's requirements.
