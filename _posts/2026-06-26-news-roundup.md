## India: DPDPA Implementation & Compliance

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has initiated the crucial process of establishing the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI) by inviting applications for its chairperson and members. This development signals the government's intent to fully operationalize the enforcement mechanism of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA).
