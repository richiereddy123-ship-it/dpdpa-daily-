## India: DPDPA Implementation and Legal Challenges

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, along with its finalized Rules 2025, has officially come into force, establishing a comprehensive legal framework for personal data protection across the nation. This significant development mandates new obligations for data fiduciaries and grants enhanced rights to data principals, reshaping how personal data is handled in India.

## Compliance and Operational Impacts

Businesses, including startups and those in the banking sector, are actively assessing the operational impacts and compliance burdens introduced by the DPDPA and its Rules. Key areas of focus include implementing robust consent mechanisms, managing data principal requests, establishing internal data governance policies, and navigating the new framework for cross-border data transfers.
