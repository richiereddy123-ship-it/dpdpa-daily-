## India: Regulatory Updates

The Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has officially opened applications for the Chairperson and members of the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI), a crucial step towards operationalizing the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA). This move signals the government's intent to establish the independent adjudicatory body responsible for enforcing the DPDPA.
